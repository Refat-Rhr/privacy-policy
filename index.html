Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

Specifically, if you are a medical professional choosing to join our platform via the "Join Doctor" feature, we collect the following information to create your public profile within the app:

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. We do not share, sell, or rent your personally identifiable information to any third parties for any purpose. The information collected through the "Join Doctor" feature is used solely to display doctor profiles within the Doctor Bangladesh app.

Specifically, sensitive information such as your **Doctor's Email, Personal Mobile Number, and BM&DC Number** collected through the "Join Doctor" form is kept confidential and is not shared publicly. Similarly, any data related to user **login and registration** is strictly for internal use and is not shared.

Most of the doctor information displayed in our app is collected from publicly available sources on Google. If you have any concerns about your information, please contact the admin.
